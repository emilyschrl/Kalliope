Kalliope

About us

Kalliope was created to host a platform and webpages for artists. People want to express themselves and need a space to easily publish and advertise their work. No matter if you write prose, express yourself in poetry or handcraft any kind of artwork - Kalliope is there to support the artist.

Kalliope is an outcome of the Vodafone Coding Girls Camp 2017 and the idea of the two young entrepreneurs Lynn Strakeljahn and Emily Scheuerle, both aged 15 that share a passoin for art.
